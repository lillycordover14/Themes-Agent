# Smith Point Capital — Investment Criteria & Network (agent reference)

This file is the human-readable companion to `data/spc_network.json` (the machine-readable source the
Sourcing Engine and any agent should load). Keep both in sync. Any automation that screens companies,
maps connections, or drafts outreach should reference this.

## What SPC invests in
Growth-stage, **mission-critical enterprise software** across four overlapping imperatives:
- **Applied AI** — autonomous agents, copilots, applied-AI for real enterprise workflows.
- **Data Management** — data quality/observability, pipelines, governance, infrastructure.
- **Edge Computing** — distributed/edge infrastructure, on-device, sovereign AI.
- **Precision Applications** — AI-native rebuilds of mission-critical vertical workflows
  (healthcare/RCM, specialty pharmacy, legal, procurement, supply chain, manufacturing/industrial,
  insurance, financial back-office/compliance, defense/geospatial).

## The bar (Investment Focus)
- ~**$20M ARR (NTM)**, revenue lifecycle roughly **$20M–$250M**; growth **>50%** (often 3x+).
- Typically **Series A–C** (growth stage); defensible **moat**; clear **path to profitability**.
- **CEO partnership** and alignment on vision; product-market fit; mission-critical / durable.
- Necessary: market-leadership potential, durable growth, mitigated execution risk.
- Avoid: no operating history, limited growth, pure technology risk, credit/leverage risk.

## What SPC repeatedly passes on
Too early / sub-$5M ARR; biotech single-bets; crowded/undifferentiated categories (generic AI-SDR,
SRE/IT agents, me-too RCM); consumer/F&B/hospitality; just-raised or over-priced rounds; anything
off-thesis thematically.

## Connection logic (what counts as a warm path)
1. **Affinity/Harmonic relationship data** — a teammate is connected (email/calendar/LinkedIn) to an exec.
2. **Co-investor** — a fund SPC knows is on the cap table.
3. **Shared experience (inference)** — any team OR advisory-network member shares a prior employer or
   school with any exec at the company (e.g., an exec ex-SAP overlaps John Wookey; ex-Vista overlaps
   Burke Norton). Name the specific SPC person.
If none: say **"No automated SPC connection surfaced"** (still worth flagging on fit).
